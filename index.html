About Me

Anna has been a teacher for 7 years. She teaches science and makerspace to k-12 students. She has a B.S. in Environmental Science, and an MFS in Creative Writing and Environment. She is currently working toward a certificate in full stack web development through Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. She is also working on her first children's book about the biology of the Chihuahuan Desert.
Anna is a life-long learner who wishes to inspire her students to love learning. She works to find innovative new ways of inpsiring her students through desgin thinking and project-based learning opportunities. Currrently, she teaches 4th grade and makerspace at a k-8 independent school in Morristown, NJ.
